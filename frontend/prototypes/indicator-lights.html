Rectangular 12×8px lamps (not circles). Click any lamp except OFFLINE to toggle asserted vs ghost state.

Labels use the same hue as their lamp (--vfd, --amber, --warning, --tai-green); ghost “off” bulbs also soften the label.

OFFLINE — --dim #555558: inert gray lens (same token as secondary UI text), not a white LED. Single read-only state; inset shadow reads recessed vs emitting colors.

Sync OK — TAI green #33FF66 for GPS / PNT / valid-time lock.

Power — cool white emitter --led-white; caption is --silk-white silkscreen ink (fixed tone + light shadow), independent of LED on/off.

Run (bubble) — small domed glass jewel over green LED (--tai-green family): radial fill, rim bezel, specular highlight. Caption is neutral panel legend (cream), not hue-linked like the flat bar lamps.

Opacity is driven with requestAnimationFrame on each bulb (works in Firefox and embedded preview). Chromatic bar-lamp labels pulse with their lamp; Power silkscreen and bubble panel legend stay fixed while only the emitters animate.

Blink — ~1.1s cycle, half bright / half dim. Strobe — short flash each 0.4s. Flicker — irregular 2.4s loop.

Honors prefers-reduced-motion (animations stop; inline opacity cleared).
